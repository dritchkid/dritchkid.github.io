The Lucky Man.

The Lucky Man is a story of Janice Lee, the lead actress in a theatre troupe in the 1910s. As a terrible con man hires the troupe, she must face her struggles with love, tyranny, and what it means to be happy.

Eve's Diary

Based on Mark Twain's short story of the same name, this take on the story of Adam and Eve is one of new love, new discoveries, and the constant fear of never being good enough, even when you're one-of-a-kind. Or, two of a kind, that is.
