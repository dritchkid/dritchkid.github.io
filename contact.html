Contact Me!

I'm David Ritch, an aspiring playwright currently working toward a degree at Texas State University. I eventually want to go into theatre management so that I can be involved in every aspect of the production process. I have written many plays and plan to write many more. Language is my second obsession, just behind theatre itself. I am currently Vice President of the Comedy Association at Texas State, where I have made many friends and many great connections.

In the meantime, add me on Twitter to see what silly thing I have to say next, and check out my writing!
